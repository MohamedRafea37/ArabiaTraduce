# Lesson 5: Family, Professions, and Describing People

Welcome to Lesson 5!

Now that you can introduce yourself and talk about everyday activities, it's time to learn how to describe people, talk about your family, and discuss occupations.

By the end of this lesson, you'll be able to:

- Talk about your family.
- Describe people.
- Say what someone does for a living.
- Use possessive adjectives like "my" and "your."

---

# Learning objectives

After completing this lesson, you will be able to:

- Name family members.
- Describe appearance and personality.
- Talk about professions.
- Use possessive adjectives correctly.

# Possessive adjectives

| English | Spanish |
|----------|----------|
| My | Mi / Mis |
| Your | Tu / Tus |
| His | Su / Sus |
| Her | Su / Sus |
| Our | Nuestro / Nuestra |
| Their | Su / Sus |

Examples:

Mi casa.

My house.

---

Your friends.

Our family.

# Using adjectives

Remember that many adjectives agree with gender and number.

Examples:

Mi hermano es alto.

My brother is tall.

---

Mi hermana es alta.

My sister is tall.

---

Mis amigos son inteligentes.

My friends are intelligent.

# Mini dialogue

### English

**A:** Do you have brothers?

**B:** Yes, I have one brother.

**A:** What does he do?

**B:** He is an engineer.

**A:** Is he friendly?

**B:** Yes, he is very friendly.

---

### Spanish

**A:** ¿Tienes hermanos?

**B:** Sí, tengo un hermano.

**A:** ¿A qué se dedica?

**B:** Es ingeniero.

**A:** ¿Es amable?

**B:** Sí, es muy amable.

# Grammar tip

## The verb **tener** (to have)

| Subject | Conjugation |
|----------|-------------|
| Yo | Tengo |
| Tú | Tienes |
| Él / Ella / Usted | Tiene |
| Nosotros | Tenemos |
| Vosotros | Tenéis |
| Ellos / Ustedes | Tienen |

Examples:

Tengo una familia grande.

I have a big family.

---

Tenemos dos hijos.

We have two children.

# Practice exercise

Translate into Spanish.

1. My father is a doctor.
2. My sister is very intelligent.
3. I have two brothers.
4. Our family is big.
5. Your mother is friendly.
6. They have three children.
7. She is an engineer.

3. Tengo dos hermanos.
4. Nuestra familia es grande.
5. Tu madre es amable.
6. Ellos tienen tres hijos.
